Obtaining an NCI account

In order to take the labs of the COMP3320/6464 course, you will need to get access to the Gadi supercomputer at NCI. Staff at the ANU are allocated time on this system through a competitive process for use in their research projects. This system is not generally available to undergraduate students; we are extremely fortunate to have been given access to this system for this course! 

Please use the machine with respect. Note that this machine is **NOT** administered or supported by the CECS IT group, you have to go via the NCI helpdesk.

The Gadi user guide (https://opus.nci.org.au/display/Help/Preparing+for+Gadi) provides basic information on the use of the machine and includes a link to an FAQ page. You should take a look at this and use it for reference throughout this course.

In order to access Gadi you need to visit the following NCI accounts website https://my.nci.org.au/mancini/login and obtain an account by following instructions below:

  • Click on the "Sign up here" option.
  • Step 1. Fill in all the boxes and click on Next.
  • Step 2. Fill in the form using your official ANU email address and all the remaining details. Go Next.
  • Step 3. Under Project affiliation select "I need to join one or more existing projects". Under Projects search for "c07" and then select "c07: Computer Science Undergraduate Course". Leave the Software access area blanc. Go Next.
  • Step 4. Under Institution select "Course Accounts, School of Computer Science, College of Engineering and Computer Science, Australian National University". Under Position write "Undergraduate Student". Go Next.
  • Step 5. Check that your details are correct and then insert and confirm your password. Click on Finish.

After following the instructions above, you will receive an email containing a link to confirm your registration.
