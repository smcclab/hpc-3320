Week 1 Checklist 

Week 1 is particularly important in this course. Please be sure to complete the following by the end of week 1:

  1. Attend both lectures.
  2. Browse the General Section of Wattle. Be sure to read the policies about assessments, extensions, and academic integrity.
  3. Sign up to the Ed Discussions forum (you will receive an invite).
  4. Apply to obtain an NCI account for using the Gadi supercomputing system.
  5. Enrol in a lab class through MyTimetable.
  6. Express your interest to become a class representative by contacting the Lecturer.
